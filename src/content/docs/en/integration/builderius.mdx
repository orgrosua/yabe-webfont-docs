[Builderius](https://builderius.io/) is a visual development environment for WordPress to create astonishing designs with an easy by the drag and drop interface.

Yabe Webfont is the pioneer of custom font solutions for Builderius. We have worked closely with the Builderius team to ensure Yabe Webfont works seamlessly with Builderius.

## Using custom fonts in the Builderius

You can use any [Custom Font](/en/font/custom-font), embedding [Adobe Fonts](/en/font/adobe-fonts) or [self-hosting Google Fonts](/en/font/google-fonts) in the Builderius seamlessly.


### Step 1: Choose the text element

Choose the text element by clicking on the canvas to open the Module settings panel on the right side of the screen.

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/integration/builderius/1.png">
![](/tutorial/integration/builderius/1.png)
</a>

### Step 2: Switch to the CSS Settings tab

Click on the CSS Settings tab to switch to the CSS Settings tab.

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/integration/builderius/2.png">
![](/tutorial/integration/builderius/2.png)
</a>

### Step 3: Open the Typography setting

Click the **Typography** menu to open the Typography setting.

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/integration/builderius/3.png">
![](/tutorial/integration/builderius/3.png)
</a>

### Step 4: Choose the font

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/integration/builderius/4.png">
![](/tutorial/integration/builderius/4.png)
</a>

1. On the **Type** dropdown list, choose `Yabe Webfont`.

2. On the **Generic family** dropdown list, choose `Yabe Webfont`.

3. Choose your font on the **Font Family** dropdown list.
